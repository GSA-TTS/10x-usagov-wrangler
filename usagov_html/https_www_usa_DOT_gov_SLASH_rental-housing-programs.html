Rental assistance

Learn about government programs that help people with low incomes find affordable rental housing. Get the details on how these programs work, who is eligible, and how to apply.

  • Section 8 housing

    A Section 8 housing choice voucher can help you pay rent for private housing. Learn if you are eligible for a voucher and how to apply.

  • Get subsidized housing

    In subsidized rental housing, the government pays apartment owners to reduce the rent for tenants with low incomes. Learn how to find this type of affordable housing.

  • Public housing

    Find out if you qualify for public housing. Learn how to locate your local public housing agency and how to apply.

  • Find rental assistance for veterans, people with disabilities, and seniors

    If you are a veteran, have a disability, or are a senior, you may qualify for rental assistance. Learn about government-backed rental assistance programs and how to apply.
