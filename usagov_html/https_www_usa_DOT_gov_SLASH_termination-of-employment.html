Termination of employment

If you feel that you have been wrongfully fired from a job or let go from an employment situation, learn about your state's wrongful discharge laws.

  • Wrongful termination

    Wrongful termination happens when your employer fires you for an illegal reason. Learn what qualifies as wrongful discharge and the actions you can take.

  • Termination guidance for employers

    Federal and state agencies have laws about how and when employees can be fired. Follow these policies and your company guidelines to avoid wrongful termination claims.
