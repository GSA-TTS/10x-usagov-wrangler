Veterans burial and survivor benefits

Learn how to qualify for and receive veterans burial and survivor benefits.

  • Military survivor benefits and life insurance

    Active duty, veterans, and retirees may be eligible to enroll in programs to provide for their survivors. Find out about military and veterans survivor benefits and life insurance.

  • Veterans death benefits

    A veterans burial allowance helps pay for transportation, funeral, and burial costs for an eligible veteran. If you qualify, the VA will repay a fixed amount for these costs.

  • Veteran burial in a national cemetery

    The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) offers burial in national cemeteries for qualifying veterans and spouses. In some cases, their child may also be eligible.

  • Military funeral honors

    Learn how the military can honor a veteran at their funeral, and find out how to get a military grave marker.
