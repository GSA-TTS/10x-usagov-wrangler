### Federal Committee on Statistical Methodology (FCSM)

Founded in 1975 within the Office of Management and Budget, FCSM helps set and coordinate statistical policy for federal agencies.

Website: [Federal Committee on Statistical Methodology](https://www.fcsm.gov/)

Contact: [Contact the Federal Committee on Statistical Methodology](https://nces.ed.gov/FCSM/contact_us.asp)

### Federal Communications Commission (FCC)

The FCC regulates interstate and international communications including cable, radio, television, satellite, and wire to promote connectivity and competitive markets.

Website: [Federal Communications Commission](https://www.fcc.gov/)

Contact: [Contact the Federal Communications Commission](https://www.fcc.gov/about/contact)

[More information about Federal Communications Commission >](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/federal-communications-commission)
