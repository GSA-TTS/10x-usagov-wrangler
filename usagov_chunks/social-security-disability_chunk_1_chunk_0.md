# SSDI and SSI benefits for people with disabilities

## SSDI and SSI concurrent benefits
## Social Security disability benefits application

**Find out if you are eligible for SSDI and apply**
---------------------------------------------------

Your eligibility for Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) is based on your age, disability, and how long you worked. To find out if you are eligible for SSDI, [use the Social Security benefits questionnaire](https://www.ssa.gov/prepare/check-eligibility-for-benefits).

Your spouse or former spouse and your children may be eligible for benefits when you start getting SSDI. [Learn about family benefits](https://www.ssa.gov/benefits/disability/family.html) and see if your family members may qualify.

You have options to apply online, by phone, or in person.

* If your application is approved, you will have a five-month waiting period for benefits to start.
* [Learn how you can appeal](https://www.ssa.gov/apply/appeal-decision-we-made) if your SSDI application is denied.

[Find out how to apply for SSDI](https://www.ssa.gov/apply?benefits=disability&age=adult)
