Help with utility bills
=======================

Learn about government programs to help pay for phone, internet, and energy bills.

* [Get help with energy bills
  --------------------------

  The LIHEAP and WAP programs help pay for heating, cooling, and home weatherization. Learn if you are eligible and how to apply. And get help if your utilities were disconnected.](/help-with-energy-bills)
* [Get help paying for phone and internet service
  ----------------------------------------------
