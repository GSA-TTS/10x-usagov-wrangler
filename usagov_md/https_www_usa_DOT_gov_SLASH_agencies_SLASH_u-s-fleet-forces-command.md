U.S. Fleet Forces Command
(USFFC)
=================================

U.S. Fleet Forces Command (USFFC) organizes, staffs, trains, maintains, and equips Navy forces, and develops fleet readiness.

-------

[U.S. Fleet Forces Command](https://www.usff.navy.mil/)
